Welcome To
MMA Alumni Trust

Welcome to the official website of the MMA Alumni Trust, a movement born from legacy, brotherhood, and a shared commitment to uplift future generations.

For more than 75 years, the historic Malabar Muslim Association Hostel has stood as a beacon of hope and opportunity for thousands of students from Malabar.

Today, the MMA Alumni Trust carries this legacy forward through education, mentorship, leadership, welfare and institution building.

Our Story

Our Heritage

The MMA Hostel has been more than a place to stay. It has been a home, a support system, and a source of inspiration for generations.

1948

The Foundation

The Malabar Muslim Association Hostel opens its doors, welcoming the first generation of students to Chennai.

1970s

A Growing Family

Hundreds of students find a home away from home, building friendships that span lifetimes.

1990s

Legacy of Support

Generations of alumni begin giving back through scholarships and mentoring younger students.

2010s

Global Connections

Alumni spread across countries, staying united by shared memories and the values of the hostel.
