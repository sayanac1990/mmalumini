About MMA Alumni Trust

We are more than a platform. We are a global community.

Through this initiative, we aim to reconnect alumni across the globe, support deserving students, create affordable hostel ecosystems, promote leadership development, and strengthen community welfare systems.

As we move ahead, our dream is not merely to celebrate the past, but to build institutions that will protect and empower future generations.
We warmly invite alumni, students, well-wishers and community members to join hands with us in this meaningful journey.
From Hostel Rooms to Board Rooms — the journey continues.
Reconnecting alumni across the globe.
Building institutions that empower generations.

Our Heritage

The MMA Hostel has been more than a place to stay. It has been a home, a support system, and a source of inspiration for generations.

1948

The Foundation

The Malabar Muslim Association Hostel opens its doors, welcoming the first generation of students to Chennai.

1970s

A Growing Family

Hundreds of students find a home away from home, building friendships that span lifetimes.

1990s

Legacy of Support

Generations of alumni begin giving back through scholarships and mentoring younger students.

2010s

Global Connections

Alumni spread across countries, staying united by shared memories and the values of the hostel.
